Hotels

Amari Phuket – Phuket

★★★★☆

Price: starting from ₹12,000–₹18,000 per night
A comfortable beachfront hotel with spacious rooms, sea views, and easy access to Patong Beach. It is great for travelers who want luxury comfort at a lower starting price.

Keemala – Phuket

★★★★★

Price:** around ₹25,000–₹35,000 per night
Famous for its treehouse-style villas, private pools, and peaceful forest setting. The location feels magical while still being close to Phuket’s beaches.

Rayavadee – Krabi

★★★★☆

Price:** around ₹35,000–₹50,000 per nightt
This resort offers beautiful cottages surrounded by limestone cliffs and tropical greenery. Its location near Railay Beach makes it one of Thailand’s most scenic stays

Six Senses Yao Noi – Koh Yao Noi

★★★★☆

Price:** around ₹45,000–₹60,000 per night
Known for eco-luxury villas with private pools and breathtaking Phang Nga Bay views. It combines peaceful island comfort with world-class wellness experiences.

Mandarin Oriental – Bangkok

★★★★☆

Cost: ₹65,000–₹90,000 per night
One of Thailand’s most iconic luxury hotels, offering elegant riverside rooms and exceptional service. Its location on the Chao Phraya River is perfect for both sightseeing and relaxation

Rosewood Phuket – Phuket

★★★★☆

Cost: ₹75,000–₹1,00,000 per night
A very stylish resort with private pool villas, a quiet beach, and top-class family comfort. It is located away from crowded Patong, giving a calm and exclusive feel.

Capella Bangkok – Bangkok

★★★★☆

Price:** around ₹90,000–₹1,20,000 per night
This ultra-luxury riverside hotel is famous for huge suites, private balconies, and personalized service. The peaceful Bangkok river location makes it feel private and grand.

Amanpuri – Phuket

★★★★☆

Price:** starting around ₹1,20,000–₹1,80,000 per night

Amanpuri is known for unmatched privacy, ocean views, and elegant Thai-style villas. Its private peninsula location offers one of the most exclusive beach experiences in Thailand.

The Siam – Bangkok

★★★★★

Price:** around ₹80,000–₹1,75,000 / night
A boutique luxury hotel with artistic interiors, river views, and a royal atmosphere. Located by the river, it mixes cultural beauty with modern comfort.

Four Seasons Tented Camp Golden Triangle – Chiang Rai

★★★★★

Price:** about ₹2,50,000+ per night
This is one of the most expensive and unique stays, with luxury tents, jungle comfort, and elephant experiences. Its remote mountain location makes it perfect for a once-in-a-lifetime trip.
