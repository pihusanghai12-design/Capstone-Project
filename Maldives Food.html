Maldivian Food

S.No Image Food Description
1 Garudhiya A traditional Maldivian fish soup made by boiling tuna with water and salt. It is usually served with rice, lime, chili, onions, and Roshi, and it is a very common meal in Maldivian homes.
2 Fihunu Mas A popular dish made from grilled fish that is marinated with chili, garlic, and spices. It has a spicy and smoky flavor and is often served with rice or roshi.
3 Bis Keemiya A famous Maldivian snack similar to a spring roll. It is filled with tuna, cabbage, onions, and boiled eggs, wrapped in thin pastry, and fried until crispy.
4 Bajiya A well-known fried snack made with a pastry shell filled with spiced tuna, coconut, and onions. It is commonly eaten as a street food or evening snack.
5 Saagu Bondibai A traditional sweet dessert made from sago, coconut milk, sugar, and cardamom. It is often prepared during festivals and special occasions in the Maldives. 🍽️🌴
