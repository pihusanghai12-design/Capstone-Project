Hotels

Arena Beach Hotel

★★★★☆

Price: starting from $50–$80 per night
This is a popular budget hotel on Maafushi Island. It offers comfortable rooms, beach views, and activities like snorkeling and island tours. It is one of the cheapest ways to experience the Maldives.

Salt Beach Hotel

★★★★★

Price:** around **$70–$120 per night**
A small beachfront hotel with basic but comfortable rooms. Visitors enjoy beach access, water sports, and nearby coral reefs.

Fihalhohi Island Resort

★★★★☆

Price:** around **$120–$200 per night
A budget-friendly island resort famous for its beautiful beaches and snorkeling spots.

Thulhagiri Island Resort

★★★★☆

Price:** around **$200–$350 per night
This resort offers beach bungalows and overwater villas with clear lagoon views.

Kurumba Maldives

★★★★☆

Price:** around **$300–$500 per night
A famous resort near the capital city Malé, with many restaurants, villas, and water sports activities.

Centara Ras Fushi Resort & Spa Maldives

★★★★☆

Price:** around **$400–$700 per night
A luxury adults-only resort known for overwater villas, spa services, and honeymoon experiences.

JOALI Maldives

★★★★☆

Price:** around **$900–$1500 per night
A very luxurious resort famous for artistic design, private pools, and high-end restaurants.

Soneva Jani

★★★★☆

Price:** starting around **$2500–$3500+ per night

This iconic resort is known for huge overwater villas with private pools and slides into the lagoon.

Cheval Blanc Randheli

★★★★★

Price:** around **$3000–$6000 per night
An ultra-luxury resort with private villas, butler service, and gourmet dining.

Soneva Secret

★★★★★

Price:** about **$5000–$10,000+ per night
One of the most expensive resorts in the Maldives with private villas, personal chefs, and extremely exclusive luxury experiences.
