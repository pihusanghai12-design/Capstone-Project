Hotels

Arethusa Hotel Athens

★★★★☆

Price: €60–70 per night
Located in the center of Athens, this budget-friendly hotel is just a short walk from famous landmarks like the Acropolis of Athens. It offers comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, breakfast service, and easy access to shops and restaurants, making it great for travelers who want a simple but convenient stay.

Amazon Hotel Athens

★★★★★

Price: ~€70–80 per night
This modern hotel sits in the lively Plaka district of Athens. It has stylish rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a breakfast buffet. The location is excellent for exploring historic sites, cafes, and museums nearby.

President Hotel Athens

★★★★☆

Price: ~€90–100 per night
A large 4-star hotel in Athens known for its rooftop swimming pool and panoramic city views. Guests can enjoy restaurants, bars, conference rooms, and comfortable modern rooms, making it popular with both tourists and business travelers.

Amalia Hotel Athens

★★★★☆

Price: ~€100–120 per night
A large 4-star hotel in Athens known for its rooftop swimming pool and panoramic city views. Guests can enjoy restaurants, bars, conference rooms, and comfortable modern rooms, making it popular with both tourists and business travelers.

Gallery Suites & Residences Piraeus

★★★★☆

Price: ~€170–180 per night
Located in Piraeus, this modern hotel offers stylish suites with kitchenettes, fitness facilities, and comfortable living spaces. It’s ideal for travelers who want easy ferry access to the Greek islands.

Stella Island Luxury Resort & Spa

★★★★☆

Price: ~€200–300 per night
Located on the island of Crete, this adults-only luxury resort is famous for its huge lagoon-style pools and overwater-style villas. Guests enjoy spa treatments, multiple restaurants, fitness facilities, and a peaceful tropical atmosphere.

Canaves Oia Suites

★★★★☆

Price: ~€500–700 per night
Perched on the cliffs of Santorini, this luxury hotel offers stunning caldera views. It features infinity pools, fine dining restaurants, spa services, and beautiful white-cave suites overlooking the sea.

Santa Marina Mykonos

★★★★☆

Price: ~€550–700 per night

Situated near Ornos Bay, this 5-star resort is famous for having the island’s only private beach. It offers luxury villas, a spa, tennis courts, beach bars, and high-end restaurants.

Grace Hotel Santorini

★★★★★

Price: ~€800–1000 per night
This boutique luxury hotel is known for its iconic infinity pool overlooking the Aegean Sea. Guests enjoy private terraces, luxury suites, fine dining, spa services, and incredible sunset views.

Amanzoe

★★★★★

Price: ~€2000–3000+ per night
Located in the hills of the Peloponnese, Amanzoe is one of the most luxurious resorts in Greece. It offers private villas with pools, world-class spa facilities, yoga pavilions, and breathtaking views of olive groves and the sea.
