Places To Visit

  1. Bangkok: Thailand’s capital is famous for its grand temples, lively street markets, and modern shopping malls. It gives a perfect mix of traditional culture and exciting city life.
  2. Phi Phi Islands:These islands are known for crystal-clear blue water, white sandy beaches, and dramatic limestone cliffs. It is one of the most beautiful places in Thailand for snorkeling and island views.
  3. Chiang Mai:Located in northern Thailand, Chiang Mai is famous for ancient temples, mountain scenery, and peaceful cultural vibes. It is the best place to experience traditional Thai heritage and nature together.
  4. Phuket:Phuket is Thailand’s largest island and is loved for its tropical beaches, nightlife, and water activities. It is perfect for both relaxation and adventure by the sea.
  5. Ayutthaya: Ayutthaya is a historic city filled with ancient temple ruins and old palaces. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site that shows Thailand’s rich royal past.
