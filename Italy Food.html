Italian Food

S.No Image Food Description
1 Pizza Margherita A classic Italian pizza made with a thin, crispy crust, fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. It is one of the most famous foods in Italy and is loved for its simple ingredients and rich cheesy taste.
2 Pasta Carbonara This is a creamy and flavorful pasta dish usually made with spaghetti, eggs, pecorino cheese, black pepper, and crispy cured meat. It has a rich, smooth texture and is especially popular in Rome.
3 Risotto Risotto is a soft and creamy rice dish cooked slowly with broth, butter, and cheese until it becomes rich and smooth. Many versions include saffron, mushrooms, vegetables, or seafood, which makes it a very versatile and delicious Italian meal.
4 Lasagna Lasagna is a baked Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, béchamel or white sauce, and lots of melted cheese. The layers blend beautifully when baked, giving it a warm, creamy, and filling taste that is perfect for family dinners.
5 Tiramisu famous Italian dessert made with coffee-soaked ladyfinger biscuits, creamy mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder on top. It has a soft, creamy texture with a sweet coffee flavor that makes it one of Italy’s most loved desserts.
