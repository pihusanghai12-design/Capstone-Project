Maldives

The Maldives is a beautiful tropical island nation located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of Sri Lanka and India. It is made up of more than 1,000 small coral islands grouped into natural atolls, which makes it one of the most unique island countries in the world.

The Maldives is famous for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, warm tropical climate, and colorful coral reefs filled with diverse marine life.

Because of its breathtaking scenery and luxury overwater resorts, the country attracts millions of tourists every year who come to relax and enjoy activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and island hopping. 🌊🏝️
