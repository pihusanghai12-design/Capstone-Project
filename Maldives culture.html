Cultures and Lifestyle

1. Traditions and Festivals

The Maldives celebrates many traditional and religious festivals. Important celebrations like **Eid al‑Fitr** and **Eid al‑Adha** are marked with prayers, family gatherings, and special meals.

2. Lifestyle and Daily Life

Life in the Maldives is peaceful and closely connected to the ocean. Many people work in **fishing, tourism, and boat-related activities**, and daily life often revolves around the sea.

3. Art and Architecture

Traditional Maldivian art includes **lacquer work, wood carving, and mat weaving**. Historic buildings such as the **Malé Friday Mosque** show beautiful coral-stone architecture and detailed carvings.

4. Music and Dance

Traditional music and dance are an important part of celebrations. A famous cultural dance is **Bodu Beru**, which uses large drums and energetic singing and dancing.

5. Food Culture

Maldivian food mainly uses **fish, coconut, and rice** because these ingredients are easily available on the islands. Many dishes are spicy and full of flavor.

6. Hospitality and Social Values

People in the Maldives are known for being **friendly and welcoming to visitors**. Hospitality and respect for guests are important parts of their culture.

7. Traditional Clothing

Women traditionally wear the **Libaas**, a long dress with beautiful embroidery, while men often wear the **Mundu**, a sarong-like garment.

8. Love for Nature and the Sea

The sea plays a very important role in Maldivian life. People value **protecting coral reefs, marine animals, and the natural beauty of the islands**. 🌊🏝️.
