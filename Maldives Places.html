Places To Visit

  1. Malé: The capital city of the Maldives and the cultural and economic center of the country. Despite being small, it is full of colorful buildings, busy markets, historic mosques, and attractions like Malé Friday Mosque and Maldives National Museum.
  2.   3. Addu City: The second largest city in the Maldives, located in the southern part of the country. It is known for its long beaches, lush greenery, and historical sites from World War II.
  4. Kulhudhuffushi: A scenic island city famous for its natural beauty and large mangrove wetlands. It is also an important commercial and transportation hub in the northern Maldives.
  5. Thinadhoo: A peaceful island city in the Gaafu Dhaalu Atoll known for its clean beaches, turquoise lagoons, and traditional Maldivian lifestyle. 🌴🌊
