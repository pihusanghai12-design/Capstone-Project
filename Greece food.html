Greek Food

S.No Image Food Description
1 Greek Salad A classic salad made with tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, feta cheese and olive oil representing the freshness of Mediterranean cuisine.
2 Moussaka A famous layered dish made with eggplant, spiced meat and creamy béchamel sauce, known as a traditional Greek comfort food.
3 Baklava A sweet pastry made of filo layers filled with nuts and honey syrup, popular across Greece and the Mediterranean.
4 Souvlaki Grilled skewers of marinated meat served with pita bread and tzatziki sauce, a popular Greek street food.
5 Spanakopita A crispy pastry filled with spinach, feta cheese and herbs, commonly enjoyed as a snack or light meal.
