Greece

Greece, a cradle of Western civilization, is a land of enchanting contrasts where ancient history and modern life intertwine. Known for its iconic whitewashed villages perched on sun-drenched cliffs, Greece invites travelers to explore a cultural tapestry that spans millennia. From the majestic Acropolis towering over Athens to the sapphire waters of the Aegean Sea, every corner of Greece tells a story. The Greek lifestyle is rooted in hospitality (philoxenia), a deep love for family, and a zest for daily pleasures—whether it’s savoring a leisurely meal, dancing at a traditional festival, or watching the sun set over Santorini. People flock to Greece not just for its postcard-perfect landscapes, but for the warmth of its culture, the richness of its history, and the vibrant spirit of its people.

3 Baklava A sweet, flaky pastry made with layers of filo, crushed nuts, and honey syrup—a dessert as rich in flavor as it is in tradition.
4 Souvlaki Grilled skewers of marinated meat, often served with pita, tzatziki, and a salad—a favorite street food that captures the essence of Greek flavor.
5 Spanakopita A golden, flaky pastry filled with spinach, feta, and herbs, enjoyed as a savory snack or light meal—beloved across Greek households.
