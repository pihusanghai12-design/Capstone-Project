Hotels

Hotel Interlaken (Interlaken)

★★★★☆

Price: starting from ~$120–200 per night
A historic hotel located in the heart of Interlaken, close to shops and train stations. It offers comfortable modern rooms with easy access to the Jungfrau region.

Hotel Bristol (Geneva)

★★★★★

Price:** around ~$180–300 per night
A stylish city hotel near Lake Geneva, known for its cozy rooms and central location. It is perfect for exploring Geneva’s attractions and lakeside promenade.

N’vY Manotel (Geneva)

★★★★☆

Price:** around ~$200–350 per nightt
A modern, artistic boutique hotel with vibrant interiors and a youthful vibe. It is located near Lake Geneva and offers easy access to the city center.

Hotel Cornavin (Geneva)

★★★★☆

Price:** around ~$200–400 per night
Located next to Geneva’s main train station, making travel extremely convenient. Rooms are simple, comfortable, and ideal for short stays.

Victoria-Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa (Interlaken)

★★★★☆

Cost: ~$400–800 per night
A luxury hotel surrounded by mountains in Interlaken with a world-class spa. It combines classic elegance with stunning Alpine views.

Beau-Rivage Genève (Geneva)

★★★★☆

Cost: ~$500–900 per night
A historic lakeside luxury hotel offering elegant rooms and refined service. It sits directly on Lake Geneva with beautiful views of the Jet d’Eau.

The Dolder Grand (Zurich)

★★★★☆

Price:** around ~$700–1,500per night
A luxury hilltop hotel overlooking Zurich with an award-winning spa. It is famous for combining modern design, art, and panoramic city views..

The Chedi Andermatt (Andermatt)

★★★★☆

Price:** starting around ~$800–1,800 per night

A high-end Alpine resort blending Swiss chalet style with Asian design. It offers ski-in/ski-out access and one of the most luxurious spas in Switzerland.

Bürgenstock Resort (Lake Lucerne)

★★★★★

Price:** around ~$900–2,000 / night
A stunning resort perched above Lake Lucerne with breathtaking views. It features infinity pools, luxury wellness centers, and world-class dining.

Badrutt’s Palace Hotel (St. Moritz)

★★★★★

Price:** about ~$1,000–3,000+ per night
One of the most iconic luxury hotels in the world, located in St. Moritz. It offers royal-level comfort, ski access, and legendary alpine luxury experiences.
