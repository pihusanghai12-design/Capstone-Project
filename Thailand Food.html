S.No Image Food Description
1 Pad Thai Pad Thai is Thailand’s most famous noodle dish, made with stir-fried rice noodles, vegetables, tofu or shrimp, and topped with peanuts. It has a perfect mix of sweet, sour, salty, and slightly spicy flavors that make it loved all over the world.
2 Tom Yum Goong This is a hot and sour soup made with shrimp, mushrooms, lemongrass, and lime leaves. Its strong spicy and tangy taste makes it one of the most iconic Thai dishes.
3 Green Curry Green curry is a creamy and flavorful dish made with coconut milk, green chilies, basil, and vegetables or chicken. It is rich, slightly spicy, and tastes amazing with steamed rice.
4 Som Tam (Papaya Salad) Som Tam is a fresh salad made with shredded green papaya, tomatoes, peanuts, and chilies. It is crunchy, spicy, sour, and sweet all at once, making it a very refreshing Thai dish.
5 Mango Sticky Rice This is a famous Thai dessert made with sweet sticky rice, fresh mango slices, and coconut milk. The soft rice with juicy mango gives it a delicious sweet tropical flavor.
